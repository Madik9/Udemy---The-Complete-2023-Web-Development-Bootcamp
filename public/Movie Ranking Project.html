My Top 3 Favorite Animated Movies

I'm a huge fan of animation so this list will focus on animated movies only.

Anything Hayao Miyazaki

So I know I just said it was only going to be three movies, but I appreciate too many of Miyazaki's movies not to include all of them. I will list my most favorites of his though, in no particular order.:

  • Spirited Away
  • Howl's Moving Castle
  • Whisper of the Heart

Chicken Little

Top tier, really under appreciated retelling of the classic story of a chicken being hit on the head with an acorn and believing the sky is falling.

The Princess and the Frog

There was one point in time where I had this entire movie memorized front to back and could recite it with correct timing and everything. Love the story, LOVE the music, and love the black main character.
